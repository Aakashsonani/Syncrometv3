Selling & Pricing

Prices are indexed to live commodity markets and adjusted for grade, cleanliness and volume. We grade transparently at weigh-in, show you how the payout is calculated, and update our reference rates regularly — synced with iScrap.
Sorting helps you get the best price, but it isn’t required. We can grade mixed loads on arrival. If you’re unsure how something classifies, bring it in or send a photo and we’ll advise.
Once you accept the graded quote, we settle quickly by the method that suits your business — cheque, e-transfer or direct deposit for commercial accounts. Large or recurring volumes can be set up on terms.

Metals & Grades

Ferrous: steel, cast iron, HMS 1 & 2, auto bodies, rail, pit scrap and prime bundles. Non-ferrous: copper, aluminum, brass, stainless, lead, zinc, nickel, catalytic converters and specialty alloys.
Ferrous metals contain iron and are magnetic (steel, iron). Non-ferrous metals don’t contain iron, resist corrosion and are non-magnetic (copper, aluminum, brass) — they generally carry higher value per pound. A simple magnet test tells them apart.
