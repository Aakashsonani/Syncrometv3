Safety & Environment

Responsible by design, safe by default

Recycling metal is one of the most impactful things an industrial business can do. We do it under strict safety controls and a genuine commitment to the environment.

Our Commitments

Three pillars guide every load we handle

Worker Safety

Trained crews, PPE, certified equipment and documented procedures. Every person on our yard goes home safe.

Environmental Care

Spill controls, contained storage and responsible handling of hazardous streams like lead and converters.

Compliance

Our practices are auditable and accountable at every step.

How We Operate

Diverting metal from landfill, the right way

Metal is infinitely recyclable — but only if it’s handled responsibly along the way. Here’s what that looks like at Syncromet.

  • Contained, surfaced storage to prevent ground contamination
  • Strict protocols for lead, converters and other hazardous streams
  • Maximised diversion — we find a buyer for grades others reject
  • Documented chain of custody on every transaction
