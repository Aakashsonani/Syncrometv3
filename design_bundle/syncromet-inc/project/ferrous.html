Ferrous Metals

Steel & iron, traded at volume

Ferrous metals contain iron and are magnetic — the backbone of construction, manufacturing and transport. We move them by the tonne with documented, transparent grading.

What We Accept

Seven ferrous grades, accurately sorted

Each load is graded to ISRI specification and weighed on certified scales. If you’re unsure how your material classifies, our team will walk you through it.

Steel Scrap

General structural and sheet steel from manufacturing and construction.

Cast Iron

Engine blocks, machine bases, pipe and heavy castings.

HMS 1 & 2

Heavy melting steel, sorted by thickness and grade.

Auto / Vehicle Bodies

End-of-life vehicles and stripped automotive hulks.

Rail / Track

Used rail, track sections and railway steel offcuts.

Pit Scrap

Mixed lower-grade ferrous recovered from yards and pits.

Prime Bundles

Clean, baled industrial sheet steel — premium ferrous grade.
