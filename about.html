About Syncromet

A metal trader you can actually trust

Family-run, certified, and Canada-wide — we’ve spent over two decades building a reputation on honest grading and fair pay.

Our Story

From a single weigh-scale to a Canada-wide trading desk

Syncromet was founded in 1998 with one weigh-scale, one truck, and a simple promise: grade every load honestly and pay a fair, market-indexed price. That promise hasn’t changed.

Today we trade ferrous and non-ferrous metals for manufacturers, contractors, demolition crews and municipalities across the country — moving tens of thousands of tonnes a year while keeping the same hands-on, relationship-first approach we started with.

We invest in certified scales, ISO-audited processes and trained graders so that what you see is exactly what you’re paid for.

Our Mission

To be Canada’s most transparent scrap metal trading partner — grading fairly, paying promptly, and diverting every possible tonne from landfill back into productive use.

Our Vision

A circular metals economy where recycled material is the first choice, not the afterthought — and where every supplier is treated as a long-term partner.
